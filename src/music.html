The dogs

rock music band

We are "The Dogs" a Rock n Roll group from Bilbao, Basque Country. Since 2017, we have been working on a better alternative to classic rock and roll. We play others groups music but giving our best. The careful selection and execution of deep cut vinyl tracks will take you back to where you were when these gems were released. No costumes, just great tunes served up by a tight musical unit who truly love what they do, and give the proper respect and reverence the music deserves.

We like to play our music anywhere, the street, in front of a small audience or in a big music concert. If we could choose, we like street the most. There we feel comfortable and can express our music at the best format. The music needs natural ways for expression, and the street and the improvisation give us that.

We haven't record any disc at the moment. The reason is simple, we like to play music in live.

We bark, but we don't bite
Yes, we make noise, we are dogs. But don't be afraid because we don't bite, we are really charmy.
We like the street
Yes, we rather than be at home. It will be easy to find us time to time playing music in any corner.
We go out at night
Yes, the day can be a great moment to play music, but our best it is given by night.
People tells us "It is easy to play someone else music". We answer "Maybe. But listen and tell us if it's better than the original".

After 2 years of steady gigs, "The Dogs" are clearly running on all cylinders and have the same enthusiasm since the first moment. With over 35 songs in the current library, "The Dogs" can easily play 2 hours without repeating a song. The list of artists runs from The Ramones, Bruce Springteen, ZZ Top, The Grateful Dead, The Cure, Beatles and Stones, to Zappa and inmaculate Fools to name a few.
